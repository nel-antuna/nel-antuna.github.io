Introduction

Purpose

This project is an app pilot whose purpose will be to monitor de performance of a series of company stocks.

In this pilot we will consider the performance of the shares of 3 Spanish banks in Ibex-35, the main Spanish index, during a period of time.

Agenda

In this presentation we will review:

  1. What data was consired
  2. How data was obtained
  3. The output of the app

The data

Stocks

Companies used for this pilot project are

  • Santander Bank
  • BBVA
  • CaixaBank

The three of them are top-3 banks in Spain.

Time span

For time reference, three periods of time have been considered:

  • Last 5 days
  • Last 15 days
  • Last month
